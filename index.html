What is a Chess Rating?

A chess rating is a system used to calculate an estimate of the strength of the player when compared to other players. However...

The Problem

A chess rating only tells you your estimated overall playing strength. One does not know where they are weak. Our app fixes that.

Positional Rating

It's Over 9000! determines your estimated playing strength during different phases of a chess game, from opening to endgame.

How We Do It

Our AI has combed through millions of games to assess the strength of any given position. Take your game to the next level, whether you're a beginner or grandmaster!
